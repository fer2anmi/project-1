The story of creation

The origin

Upon a time,in a vast emptiness of space there was only God. God,the loving and all-powerful creator,had a magnificent plan in mind. with a divine thought, God set in motion the creation of a beautiful world.

God fashioned the heavens and the earth and celestial bodies that would provide light. Next He created a breathtaking array of flora and fauna,with living creature and his crown work he created animals and mankind and put them in charge of everything. However they rebelled by disobeying God.

However God in his infinite mercy and love sent his only son to die for the remission of sin an bring hope,peace and redemption to mankind. And so the story of God's creation Continues to unfold day-to-day may this story inspire us to love and cherish the creator
